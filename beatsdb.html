Learn More

BeatsDB is a crowd-sourced database of hip hop instrumentals. Share your favorite nuances of songs, or learn more about hip hop music. Have you ever wondered how a producer created a certain sound? Come to BeatsDB to see the source, as well as an educated, detailed explanation.

This project is currently under development. Right now, I (Greg Kim) am the only developer, but once basic functionality is finished, I plan to open source the project. Feel free to contact me if you're interested in helping, and please refer to this page in the coming weeks for updates.

In the meantime, try out the proof-of-concept demo. Everything about it is subject to change; I am still in the process of testing and determining the best layout and style.
